Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to Perfect Price. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Perfect Price browser extension is designed to track product prices on eMAG. To provide this service, the extension collects the following information when you visit product pages on eMAG.ro:

  • Product Information: URLs, product titles, and current prices.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, browsing history outside of eMAG, or payment information through the browser extension.

2. How We Use Your Information

The product data collected is transmitted to our secure server to:

  • Maintain historical price records for products.
  • Display price history charts directly on eMAG product pages.
  • Send email alerts to registered users when a tracked product's price drops.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. The product pricing data is aggregated and used solely to provide the price tracking functionality to our users.

4. User Accounts

If you choose to create an account on our web dashboard or desktop application to set up personalized price drop alerts, we will securely store the email address you provide solely for the purpose of sending you those alerts.

5. Your Consent

By using the Perfect Price browser extension, you consent to our privacy policy.
